How the buttons work
Strike = any called or swinging strike (advances the count; 3 = strikeout).
Ball = out of the zone (4 = walk).
Foul = counts as a strike thrown for Strike %, but won't strike the batter out at 2 strikes.
In Play = ball put in play; counts as a strike thrown and ends the at-bat.
Next Batter = end the at-bat for a walk, hit-by-pitch, or any out without logging another pitch.
Undo reverses the last action. K/BB auto-tally on the 3rd strike / 4th ball.
